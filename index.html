Nexus Kitchen Group connects manufacturers, people, and ideas.

Driven by a shared vision, complementary brands share experience, technology, and opportunity, enabling each to grow stronger while staying true to its craft.

Our model is simple: we connect factories to empower growth. We combine product ranges and know-how, apply economies of scale, and keep quality at the center. By staying close to the markets we serve, investing locally, and building strong teams, we create professional kitchen solutions that are reliable, efficient, and built to last.

Nexus Kitchen Group was created with a simple belief, that the future of our industry belongs to those who build together. Every brand in our group carries its own heritage, yet our strength comes from uniting knowledge, talent, and capability under one vision. We focus on engineering excellence, investing locally, and creating systems that serve chefs, operators, and partners with reliability they can trust. Our goal is not only to manufacture equipment, but to build a lasting heritage, one that grows, adapts, and elevates every brand within it. Nexus Kitchen is our commitment to a better way of building the kitchen industry’s future.

Collaboration

We connect complementary strengths, align roadmaps, and share what works. Openness accelerates progress, reduces duplication, and lets each brand focus on what it does best, while the whole system moves forward together.

Shared Knowledge

Expertise grows when it is transferred. We document, teach, and mentor across teams and countries, so skills, standards, and improvements are replicated consistently and raise the bar for everyone.

Future Factories Vision

We plan and invest for tomorrow by building production closer to our markets. Local factories reduce transport, strengthen supply resilience, and develop skilled teams, supported by digital solutions, smarter energy use, and infrastructure designed to scale.

Sustainability

We aim to reduce waste and miles, prioritize durable engineering, and build supply closer to demand. Local production, efficient logistics, and long-life products are the foundation for responsible growth.

Our Team

Behind every brand is a team of specialists, engineers, and leaders who focus on their own expertise while working side by side on shared projects. Their combined knowledge and dedication turn every project into a reflection of the group’s collective strength. Our leadership team builds on this foundation, bringing together decades of experience in professional kitchen manufacturing, project execution, and operational excellence.

They are united by a shared purpose: to engineer growth through collaboration and build a global manufacturing alliance.

NORTH

Refrigeration

With decades of industry experience and close ties to European suppliers, North has grown from humble assembly production into the only manufacturer in the region fully dedicated to commercial refrigeration, supported by in-house foaming and jig capabilities across its entire refrigeration range. Its products are engineered for performance, durability, and efficiency.

KOPA

Grilling Solutions

Founded in 2014 in Slovenia, with advanced production facilities spanning over 6,000 square meters and strong in-house manufacturing capabilities, KOPA combines traditional grilling techniques with modern engineering. Each solution is tailored to the chef’s vision, delivering efficient, reliable, and visually distinctive cooking performance.

AVANTE

Ventilation Systems

With over 50 years of collective engineering experience across the UK, the Gulf, and India, Avante delivers innovative ventilation, filtration, and fire protection solutions. Based in Dubai Investment Park 2 in the UAE, Avante’s 25,000 sq. ft. facility ensures full in-house production, stringent quality standards, and custom-engineered systems in kitchen extract ventilation, HVAC, demand-based ventilation, and fire suppression.

DARC

Cold Creations

DARC produces state-of-the-art refrigerated display solutions that preserve food at optimal conditions while becoming an integral part of a venue’s interior design. Backed by deep refrigeration expertise, DARC focuses on innovation in design, craftsmanship, and customization, delivering high-end showcases that combine performance with refined aesthetics.

KOOK

Cooking Suites

KOOK is a Slovenian manufacturer specializing in bespoke cooking suites and stainless steel fabrication designed for Front-of-House spaces. With 20 years of expertise, KOOK develops professional kitchen blocks that integrate performance, design precision, and operational efficiency into cohesive monoblock systems, supporting elevated culinary experiences.

KARV

Outdoor Living

KARV focuses on outdoor living solutions that combine craftsmanship, design, and functionality. Originating in Slovenia and shaped by years of expertise in professional grilling solutions, KARV has evolved into a refined outdoor brand. Its approach balances clarity of design, functional simplicity, and refined flair, delivering both fixed and mobile solutions that elevate outdoor spaces for residential and professional hospitality environments.
